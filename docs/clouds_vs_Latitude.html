Cloudiness vs Latitude


This is a scatter plot showing cloudiness as a funciton of latitude. Latitude is defined as the angular distance of a place north or south of the earth's equator, or of a celestial object north or south of the celestial equator, usually expressed in degrees and minutes. Looking at the distribution of this plot we see no noticeable patterns between latitude and cloudiness. There is a higher frequency of occurences in the northern hemisphere but that just means that more latitudes were recorded in the data set. This shows that there is no significant relation between latitude and cloudiness
